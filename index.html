My work

My primary focus revolves around designing and creating affordable solutions for air pollution monitors tailored to Indian weather conditions. In addition to product design, I have a keen interest in data analysis and exploring innovative visualization techniques to simplify complex datasets.

Personal Exposure Monitor

We engineered and created a compact, cost-effective exposure monitor capable of measuring PM2.5, CO2, temperature, and humidity. Additionally, the device features geotagging functionality, allowing users to correlate their exposure with specific spatiotemporal locations.

Outdoor PM2.5 Monitor

We successfully developed a budget-friendly PM2.5 monitor priced at under $150 USD. These monitors have undergone rigorous testing in various ambient conditions and extreme exposure scenarios over a period of more than three years.

UAGV Prototype

For my M.tech Thesis, I developed a compact Unmanned Autonomous Ground Vehicle (U.A.G.V.) equipped with a rocker bogie mechanism and a stable platform suitable for tracking and various other applications.

LCS calibration

We work closely with DPCC and CPCB to calibrate our low cost monitors with reference.

Collocated Study

To comprehend the impact of climatic conditions and exposure on lifespan, reliability, and other parameters, we deployed our monitors in various climatic regions for collocation studies.

Data Analysis

We create custom dashboards for real-time data monitoring and historical data analysis using both R Studio and Python.
