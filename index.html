Gulshan Kumar

Ph.D. Candidate

Biography

Passionate about low-cost air pollution sensors and democratizing access to environmental data for communities worldwide. As a mechanical engineer deeply committed to tackling real-world challenges, my journey has evolved to embrace the integration of mechatronics and human health. This multidisciplinary background paves the way for data-driven, human-centered solutions with tangible, real-world applications.

Currently a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Design at IIT Delhi, my primary focus revolves around designing and creating affordable solutions for air pollution monitors tailored to diverse weather conditions.

Research Interests

  • Product Design for Environmental Monitoring
  • Human-Centered Personal Exposure Monitors
  • Interaction Design & Data Visualization
  • Embedded Systems & Open-Source Hardware
  • Data-Driven Approaches for Sensor Performance Evaluation, Calibration and Drift Detection.
